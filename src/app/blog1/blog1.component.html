Do you want to become a Developer?

Introduction

Ever been astonished and astounded looking at the obtainable choices for a human being, even after making an immense decision of setting up the carrier in the Technological field?

Do not worry, I am just here to tell you that you are not alone!

There are ample number of languages and frameworks available out there in the Tech-world and a person working his way out into the Tech-world cannot and should not focus on all the available options. He/She should weigh the opportunities and needs at the particular time and make a wise decision by considering the future path also that He/She wants to have in the Tech-World.

Even Though, development in Technology is very difficult, one of the main advantages available in the Tech-World would be that any developer understanding a programming language, will be able to understand other programming languages in an easier manner, as mostly the differences would be syntax and keywords only. Thus, making a proper decision is vital at the beginning but will not become a disadvantage in any manner.

Choosing over Mobile Apps or Web Apps

The first and foremost decision to be made even before choosing the languages and framework would be choosing the platform you would like to develop on. Most common choices would be Web Developer or Native Developer?

My suggestion would be a Web Developer. Why? Compared to Mobile development web development is way easier to learn and understand in the initial stages, but as mentioned before if the needs and opportunities thrive you towards Mobile development then start your carrier and knowledge acquisition in Mobile development. If you progress towards Mobile development you would have to learn the languages according to the previously defined frameworks for the major Mobile Softwares such as Android Studio/XCode IDE.

Another additional point from my side to choose Web development would be the upcoming concept known as “Web Apps”. As they are cross-platform available and can be developed using technologies and have very few drawbacks when compared to Native development.

Another important point to be considered would be the businesses out there prefer cross-platform development more than native development based long-term investment.

Does salary obtained depend on Technology?

You will not be able to find huge differences in salary-wise here. In comparison between different developers the conclusion was that Data Science and Game Developers are obtaining higher salaries than other developers. Other than that all other developers such as Frontend Developer, Full Stack Developer and Native Developer are getting paid in the same range. Basically, the difference in salary is created only because of the Company and Experience and not the Technology mostly.

Conclusion

1) As a fresher, choosing the Technology to learn and working initially is a vital thing.

2) Work on selected technology for at least 2 years for a deeper understanding.

3) Work experience is an essential path for deeper knowledge and experience.

4) Once you have attained considerable experience, switching languages and frameworks will not be a very difficult task.

5) Choose data science and game development based-related fields to earn higher.

6) Experience and Company environment are essential ones for attaining knowledge and gradual development in the Money stream eventually.
